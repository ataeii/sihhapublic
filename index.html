Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Sihha Fit → Cancel.

How do I restore my purchases?
Open the app → Profile → Upgrade to Premium → Restore Purchases.

My workout didn't save. What do I do?
Make sure you tap "Finish & Save" at the end of your workout session. If the issue persists, contact us.

Can I delete my account?
Email us at ataeii@gmail.com with your request and we'll permanently delete your account and all data within 48 hours.
